Ink Favicons

Floyd-Steinberg dithered
T
Serif initial fallback

Domain favicons are loaded at 32px, converted to greyscale (ITU-R: 0.299R + 0.587G + 0.114B), then Floyd-Steinberg dithered to 1-bit monochrome. The result renders at 16×16 with image-rendering: pixelated. When the favicon fails to load, a serif italic initial letter is shown instead.

Ink-Wash Thumbnails

Hover to reveal colour

Thumbnails are rendered to a canvas at max 176px width, desaturated to greyscale, then posterised to four tonal levels: [0, 85, 170, 255]. A 1px CSS blur softens the banding. The original full-colour image sits on top with opacity: 0, fading in on hover over 0.3s.

Reader Sidenotes & Breath Marks

saved 14 feb 2026
1,240 words
6 min read

The reader view uses a Tuftean margin layout. Metadata sits in the left column at small-caps, 0.75rem, in a muted colour. The main content column uses Georgia at 1.125rem with 1.8 line-height.

Breath marks are tiny 1px × 8px vertical ticks in the margin, recording where the reader paused. Opacity ranges from 0.12 (oldest) to 0.34 (most recent), with a maximum of five marks per article. Hidden on mobile; the grid activates at ≥1080px.

Marks are stored per-article in localStorage as scroll-position percentages (0–1). A new mark is saved on unmount if the reader paused ≥2% away from the last mark.

Audio Player

Fixed to the viewport bottom at z-index: 200. Play button is 42×42 with accent fill; skip buttons are 36×36. Progress bar is 4px tall, no border-radius. Speed cycles through [0.75, 1, 1.25, 1.5, 1.75, 2]×. Integrates with the OS Media Session API.

Offline Indicator

Saved offline

18×18 badge with 2px border-radius. Background is #5a7068 (the desaturated success green), white check icon. Appears on article cards when the article is cached by the service worker.

Tag Rules

tech Domain arstechnica.com
essays Title contains on writing

Auto-tagging form: tag select + match-type select + pattern input, flex-wrapped. On mobile (≤639px), the form stacks vertically. Each existing rule displays as a row: tag chip (small-caps) + secondary match-type label + monospace pattern.

Processing States

Cards in pending or processing status show a desaturated overlay with a marching-ants border. All action buttons (star, delete, archive, audio) are hidden until the article resolves to ready or failed. The app polls every 5 seconds and restores buttons automatically on resolution.

Reader Action Groups

Reader actions are grouped into three logical clusters separated by 1px vertical dividers: status actions (favourite, reading status, offline), audio actions (listen, download, request), and meta/destructive actions (original, retry, delete). On mobile (≤639px) the dividers are hidden and groups wrap naturally.

Bulk Action Bar

Selection controls (count, Select All, Clear) group on the left; destructive actions (Archive, Delete, Close) are pushed right with margin-left: auto.

Design Principles

Near-monochrome ink

Almost no saturated colour. Light theme is near-black on white, dark theme inverts. Semantic colours are desaturated earth tones.

Printed, not digital

Sharp corners (2px radius), no shadows, heavy typographic borders, small-caps metadata, serif headlines. The UI reads like a broadsheet.

British English

All user-facing text uses British spelling: favourite, colour, organisation. CSS class names remain American (industry convention).

Content first

Reader view uses Georgia at 1.125rem/1.8 line-height with hanging punctuation and optical kerning. The UI disappears when reading.
